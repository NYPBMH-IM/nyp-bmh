Educational & Conference Reimbursement

Educational Allowance: $2,000 per year. Unused funds rollover to the following academic year. Must be used prior to leaving the program.

Educational allowance cannot be used for electronics (cell phones, laptops, iPads) or clothing (scrubs, sneakers, etc).

Conference Presentations:Submit the CME time-off form at least 60 days in advance. Obtain APD signature and attach supporting documents.

Conference Reimbursement — what's covered:

  • Registration fee, hotel (max $300/night for conference dates only), flight
  • Poster/abstracts, Ubers to/from airport and conference
  • Food (max $30/day)

Upon return, email all receipts to Donshanell Lawrence, CC: Janine Duncan and Tasha Briggs.

Make sure Donshanell Lawrence and Tasha Briggs are your delegates in Concur so reimbursement can be processed.

UpToDate Access

  1. Go to a hospital-networked computer with open Internet access
  2. Visit uptodate.com — you'll see "NewYork Presbyterian Healthcare Systems" in the upper right corner
  3. Select Register to create a new account
  4. You can now access UpToDate remotely including via the mobile app
Every 90 days, UpToDate requires you to re-verify from a hospital-networked computer. Log in on hospital WiFi to re-certify for another 90 days.

Meal Card Balance

Each resident receives $2,000/year in meals. This does not roll over into the following year.

Meals purchased outside the hospital (at La Bagel or the Donut Shop) using your annual meal allowance are considered taxable income by the IRS.

Imprivata Troubleshooting

Imprivata passwords need to be reset every 30 days.

  1. Go to infonetbrooklyn.nyp.org
  2. Hover over the Technology tab → select "Self Service Password Reset"
  3. Log in with your username and old password
  4. Change your password

For user-specific issues: NYP Help Desk 212-746-4357

How to Add a Printer

  1. Download PrinterLogic
  2. Go to printers.nyp.org
  3. Find your printer, click it — a new window will open
  4. Check the checkbox and click Yes to install
