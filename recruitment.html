About the Program

NewYork-Presbyterian Brooklyn Methodist Hospital's Internal Medicine Residency Program provides comprehensive training in a high-volume, academically rigorous environment. Our residents care for a diverse patient population across inpatient, outpatient, and intensive care settings, supported by expert faculty and a strong community of co-residents.

Program Highlights

  • Affiliated with NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital system
  • High-volume, diverse patient population in Brooklyn
  • Strong didactic curriculum with weekly conferences
  • Robust research and quality improvement opportunities
  • Dedicated wellness and mentorship programs
  • ACGME-accredited program

Benefits & Compensation

  • Competitive salary commensurate with PGY level
  • Health, dental & vision insurance (CIR)
  • $2,000 annual educational allowance
  • $2,000 annual meal allowance
  • Parental leave benefits
  • Retirement plan (NYP Brooklyn)
  • Life insurance & disability coverage
  • Conference/travel reimbursement

Training Rotations

  • General Internal Medicine (inpatient)
  • Medical Intensive Care Unit (MICU)
  • Cardiology
  • Neurology
  • Ambulatory/Outpatient Clinic
  • Elective rotations
  • Night float & jeopardy coverage

Educational Curriculum

  • Weekly grand rounds
  • Morning report & intern report
  • Board review sessions
  • Journal club
  • Morbidity & mortality conference
  • Quality improvement projects
  • Monthly in-training exams
  • Telemetry teaching series
